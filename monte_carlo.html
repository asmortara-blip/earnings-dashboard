Methodology

Each simulation draws 200 trade returns uniformly at random (with replacement) from the 89 actual paper trade P&L percentages observed during Phase 3. Position sizing is fixed at 5% of current equity, allowing for geometric compounding. This bootstrap approach preserves the empirical return distribution without assuming normality, capturing the fat tails and skew inherent in momentum/retail-sentiment trades.

Limitations: Assumes trade returns are i.i.d. (no serial correlation or regime dependence). Does not account for position limits, margin requirements, or market impact at larger sizes. Past edge may decay over time.

RS Strategy Impact

Shipping Weakness→Supply Chain Disruption
Falling shipping rates signal demand slowdown or port congestion. Disruption risk raises input costs, pressures margins, and elevates inflation expectations.
Inflation Risk→VIX Spike
Supply chain stress feeds into CPI/PPI prints. Inflation surprises drive rate-hike fears, pushing VIX higher and triggering the RS macro dampener (0.70x when VIX >28).
Shipping Strength→Risk-On Macro
Rising freight rates and strong transports signal healthy global trade. Risk-on environment is favorable for meme/momentum names in the RS universe.
IYT vs SPY→Leading Indicator
Transport outperformance vs SPY historically leads broader market. IYT weakness diverging from SPY is an early warning of macro deterioration.

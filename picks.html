Methodology

Each of the 40 candidates in the DNSD RS universe is scored using a composite pick score model. The model combines five factors to identify tickers with the highest probability of generating positive returns on the next RS-triggered entry signal.

FactorWeightDescription
Historical Win Rate30%Paper trade win rate from rs_monitor.db (most predictive)
Base Resonance (RES)25%Retail engagement score 0-100 from candidate metadata
Short Amplifier (SA)25%Squeeze potential: SI%, borrow rate, SI velocity, FINRA short volume
Trade Frequency10%Number of historical trades (capped at bonus of 20)
Average P&L10%Mean return per trade (positive only; negative contributes zero)

Price data and company fundamentals are sourced from Yahoo Finance. Short interest data is maintained in si_overrides.json, updated weekly via rs_update_si.py with daily FINRA short volume supplements. All trade statistics derive from paper trading in Phase 3 of the strategy lifecycle.
